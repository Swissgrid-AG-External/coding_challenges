# Hiring challenge for Product Owner - Software Engineering

## Introduction

Swissgrid named you the official Product Owner for Software Engineering, congratulations!

Swissgrid has around 400 developers who are all on GitHub and are working on countless projects.
The knowledge level of developers is varying a lot, ranging from full time developers who do nothing else to 
people who only use GitHub and developer environments once a month.
As a rule of thumb you can assume that 80% of these 400 people don't develop fulltime.
The company also has a "Reuse before buy before make" policy meaning that most of the work is configuring/extending bought in products instead of developing them from scratch. 
Swissgrid developers use both on premises development environments as well as cloud development environments for their work.

## First task
With the cloud development environments all developers who use them are happy.
This is not the case with the on premises development environment where developers are giving the following problems as feedback:

- Development environment is highly restrictive, additional software packages take sometimes two weeks to get approved. This frustrates many developers.
- Processes for approvals take a lot of time, both managers who have no idea what they are approving are complaining as well as requester waiting until they get their request implemented.
- Development machines are slow due to a lot of installed software on each machine. Machines are relatively small with a maximum of 8 cores CPU and 8 GB of RAM. 
- Software constantly breaks or is not working anymore due to updates of versions.
- Software Owners don't test their changes in time and don't take responsibility for their software. 

## Deliverables

Please think about these problems and communicate in a 10 min pitch to the management a prioritized action plan how to solve them.
Communicate with whatever tool you are comfortable in, nothing is forbidden.

**Please don't spend more than 4 hours on this problem, we will focus more one methodology, thought process & communication than actual delivery**

## Evaluation criteria:
- Pragmatism
- Addressing problems
- Clear priorities
- Clear communication
- Clear chain of thoughts

Technical approaches are not so important although if you can bring great technical solutions please let us know!
