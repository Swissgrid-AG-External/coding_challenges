# Hiring challenge for Product Owner - Software Engineering

## Introduction

Swissgrid named you the official Product Owner for Software Engineering in the Business Unit Market, congratulations!

Swissgrid has around 400 developers who are all on GitHub and are working on countless projects.
The knowledge level of developers is varying a lot, ranging from fulltime developers who do nothing else to 
people who only use GitHub and developer environments once a month.

## First task
Your first task consists of an prioritized action plan how to solve the problems.

The problems are the following:
- Development environment is highly restrictive, additional software packages take sometimes two weeks to get approved. This frustrates many developers.
- Processes for approvals take a lot of time, both managers who have no idea what they are approving are complaining as well as requesters waiting until they get their request implemented.
- Development machines are slow due to a lot of installed software on each machine. Machines are relatively small with a maximum of 8 cores CPU and 8 GB of RAM. 
- Software constantly breaks or is not working anymore due to updates of versions.
- Software Owners don't test their changes in time and don't take responsibility for their software. 


Evaluation criteria:
- Addressing problems
- Clear priorities
- Clear communication

Technical stuff is not so important although if you can bring great technical solutions please let us know! 
## Deliverables

Please think about this problem and communicate in a 10 min pitch to the management how you would tackle it.
Communicate with whatever tool you are comfortable in, nothing is forbidden.
